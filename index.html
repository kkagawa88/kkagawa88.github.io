Vegify

This project is from my recently finished capstone. Basically vegify is a tool for restaurants to easily explore vegan food substitutions. Vegify can parse uploaded menus, run that information against a database of vegan food alternatives, and generate customized suggestions for food alternatives. This also includes an email utility that can generate marketing emails to link to the generated customer cart. To see more, click here!
